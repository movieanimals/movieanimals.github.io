About Movie Animals

Movie Animals is a professional animal training and film services company based in Palmdale, California, with a rich family history in the film industry. Our founder, Derek Toth, is a distinguished third-generation animal trainer with deep-rooted family connections in the field. Nurtured by his grandfather's legacy as a master falconer and founder of a prominent animal training company, Derek was immersed in an environment that fueled his passion for wildlife, environmental conservation, and animals from a young age.

His career commenced early, working with animals in various media productions and following in his family's footsteps. Derek gained extensive experience in caring for and training a wide array of wild and domestic species, contributing to the family's enduring legacy in the motion picture industry.

After obtaining an Associate in Arts degree with a focus on Philosophy, Derek explored various roles, including working at a renowned film studio as a Technical Asset Auditor/Data Analyst and as a dog trainer/groomer for high-profile clientele. His part-time endeavors encompassed animal caretaking for a respected organization, paving the way for his eventual full-time career as a studio animal trainer and snake wrangler. Today, he boasts an impressive record with thousands of hours of set experience.

With over a decade of expertise in animal training for film, video, and photography projects, Derek is a seasoned professional possessing a wealth of knowledge in on-set animal handling. His credits span commercials, TV shows, films, and video games for major brands and productions. Furthermore, Derek has honed his skills in venomous snake management and relocation during location shoots. Outside of work, he enjoys backcountry hiking and volunteering for animal educational programs. Derek's commitment to his passion is evident as he continues to make a mark in the entertainment industry with his balanced approach and dedication to the craft.

Our Services

Our professional pet grooming and boarding services provide top-notch care for your furry companions, whether they need a fresh haircut, a pampering bath, or a home away from home while you're on vacation.
